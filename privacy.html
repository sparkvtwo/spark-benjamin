Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 3, 2026

1. Introduction

v.two ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Spark Benjamin application. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

By using the Software, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account & User Information

When you register or use the Software, we may collect your name, email address, company name, and authentication tokens.

2.2 QuickBooks Financial Data

With your explicit authorization, the Software connects to your QuickBooks account and may access vendor records, software-related expense transactions, invoice and bill data relevant to license tracking, and chart of accounts categories. We access only the data necessary to provide license expense tracking. We do not access payroll data, personal employee financial data, or unrelated financial records.

2.3 Usage Data

We may automatically collect log data (IP address, browser type, timestamps), device information, and feature usage patterns to improve the Software.

2.4 Communications

If you contact us for support, we retain records of that correspondence.

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 Intuit / QuickBooks

The Software integrates with QuickBooks via Intuit's APIs. When you authorize this connection, Intuit's own Privacy Policy and Terms of Service govern Intuit's handling of your data. v.two is an independent developer and is not affiliated with Intuit.

4.2 Infrastructure & Hosting

We may use reputable third-party providers for cloud hosting, data storage, and analytics. These providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and process it only as directed by us.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data: Retained for the duration of your account, plus up to 90 days after termination.
  • QuickBooks financial data: Cached data deleted within 30 days of account closure.
  • Usage logs: Retained up to 12 months, then deleted or anonymized.
  • Support communications: Retained up to 2 years.

6. Your Rights

You may have the following rights depending on your location:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Portability: Request your data in a portable format.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential communications at any time.
  • Revoke QuickBooks Access: Revoke access at any time through your Intuit account settings.

To exercise these rights, contact us at chris@vtwo.co. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest, access controls, and regular security reviews. No system is completely secure — we encourage the use of strong, unique passwords for your account.

8. Children's Privacy

The Software is intended for business use only and is not directed to individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last Updated" date above. For significant changes, we will notify you via email or a prominent notice within the Software.
