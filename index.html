Capabilities
Everything you need to manage expenses
🔌

QuickBooks Integration

Connect your QuickBooks account in one click. Spark automatically pulls your expense transactions and keeps them in sync.

📊

License Tracking

Automatically identify software subscriptions across your transactions. Know exactly what you're paying for, and when it renews.

💬

Conversational Reports

Ask Spark anything — "What are we spending on SaaS tools?" or "What renews next month?" — and get instant, clear answers.

🔔

Renewal Alerts

Never get surprised by a renewal again. Spark flags upcoming renewals so you can decide whether to keep, cancel, or renegotiate.

📁

Expense Categorization

Transactions automatically categorized by type — Dev Tools, Infrastructure, Productivity, AI, and more.

OAuth2 authorization means your credentials are never stored. You can revoke access at any time from your QuickBooks account.
